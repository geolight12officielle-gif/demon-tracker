# demon-tracker
a demon tracker for Geometry Dash
# 📖 How to use GD Progress Tracker

## 🚀 Launching the app

1. Double-click the `index.html` file
2. It opens in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge…)
3. That's it — no installation, no internet required

> Your data is saved automatically in your browser. As long as you don't clear the cache, it stays there.

---

## ✅ Before you play — Checklist

On first launch you land on the **Checklist**.

- Check each item to confirm you're ready
- The banner at the top turns **green** when everything is checked
- To add your own task: click **➕ Add a task**, type its name and confirm
- To delete a task: hover over it, a **✕** button appears

---

## ➕ Adding a demon

1. Go to the **🎮 Demons** tab
2. Fill in the form at the top:
   - **Name** of the level
   - **GDDL** (1–39) → the difficulty shows automatically
   - **Status**: *In Progress* or *Completed*
   - The other fields (attempts, %, time, comment) are optional
3. Click **Add**

---

## ⏳ Tracking a demon in progress

Each *In Progress* demon has a progress block with 4 sections:

### 📊 General progress
- Update your **max %** and the number of **attempts added** since your last session
- Click **💾 Save** → the progress bar updates
- If you reach 100%, the demon is automatically marked **Completed** 🎉

### 🏃 Practice runs (segments)
To record portions of the level you're working on (e.g. 75% → 100%):
- Enter the **start %** and the **end %**
- Click **➕ Add**
- A **coverage map** builds automatically showing which zones you've worked the most (orange → gold → cyan → green based on number of passes)
- If you do the same segment multiple times, it shows once with a **×N** badge

### 👑 Full runs (practice)
To record each time you finish the level from 0 to 100% in practice mode:
- Enter the **number of attempts** used for this run
- Add a **note** if you want (optional)
- Click **➕ Add**
- The ranking sorts automatically from **fewest attempts** (best) to most
- The PB is highlighted with a crown 👑

### 💀 Noclip Runs
To track your progress in noclip (fewer deaths = better level knowledge):
- Enter the **number of deaths** in your run
- Add a **note** if you want (optional)
- Click **➕ Add**
- A **graph** appears from 2 runs onward to show whether you're improving (green bars = below your average)
- The app automatically shows 📈 *Improving*, 📉 *Needs work* or ↔️ *Stable*

---

## ✔️ Marking a demon as completed

On the demon card, click **✅ Mark as completed** — it moves to the completed demons list with today's date.

---

## 📝 Session journal

In the **📝 Notes** tab:
- Click **➕ New note**
- Write what you worked on, your feelings, your goals
- You can add **checkable goals** to your note
- Notes are sorted by date

---

## 📊 Statistics

The **Statistics** tab automatically shows:
- Your counters by difficulty
- Your personal records (hardest, most attempts, etc.)
- Progress charts

---

## ⚙️ Settings

In the **⚙️ Settings** tab you can:
- Add a custom **background image**
- Play **background music** with volume control
- **Export** your data as JSON (to back it up)
- **Reset** all data (irreversible action)

> 💡 **Tip**: export your data regularly via *Settings → Export data*. If you clear your browser cache, everything will be lost.
